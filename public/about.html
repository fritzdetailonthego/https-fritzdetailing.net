The guy behind the shine. Bringing professional mobile detailing directly to your door across Southwest Virginia.

The Story

Fritz started detailing cars out of a genuine passion for making vehicles look their absolute best. What began as a side hustle quickly turned into a full-fledged mobile detailing business serving the entire Southwest Virginia area.

With Fritz's Detail on the Go, the mission is simple — deliver showroom-quality results without you ever having to leave your driveway. Using only premium products and proven techniques, every detail is done by hand with the kind of care you'd give your own ride.

From a quick premium wash to a full ceramic coating, Fritz brings the equipment, the expertise, and the attention to detail that makes the difference. No shortcuts. No rush jobs. Just clean cars and happy customers.
