The Story

Fritz runs a mobile detailing business out of Chilhowie, Virginia. He comes to you. Cars, trucks, SUVs, motorcycles, ATVs, golf carts, dirt bikes. If it's got paint, he'll detail it.

Everything is done by hand with professional products. No automated car wash brushes, no shortcuts. Fritz works weekends and covers most of Southwest Virginia.

Products We Trust

We only use professional-grade products that deliver real results. No cheap alternatives, no cutting corners.

Ceramic Coatings
Professional-grade SiO2 ceramic coatings for long-lasting paint protection, hydrophobic finish, and UV resistance.
pH-Balanced Soaps
Gentle on paint, tough on dirt. Our wash soaps won't strip wax or sealant — they clean without damage.
Microfiber Mitts & Towels
Premium microfiber for scratch-free washing, drying, and buffing. We never use sponges or rags.
Dual Action Polishers
Machine polishing for paint correction — removes swirls, scratches, and oxidation safely and effectively.
Clay Bar Kits
Removes embedded contaminants, iron fallout, and industrial particles for silky-smooth paint before wax or coating.
Interior Cleaners & Conditioners
Leather conditioners, fabric cleaners, and dashboard protectants that restore and protect your cabin.
