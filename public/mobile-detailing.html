How Mobile Detailing Works

Fritz's Detail on the Go is 100% mobile. Every service is performed at your location. Fritz arrives with a fully equipped mobile detailing setup including water supply, power, professional tools, and premium products. All you need is a parking spot.

1. Text or Call

Reach out to Fritz at (276) 247-0921 with your vehicle type and what services you need. Get an instant quote.

2. Schedule

Pick a date and time that works for you. Fritz works around your schedule: mornings, afternoons, or weekends.

3. We Come to You

Fritz arrives at your home, office, or any location with everything needed. You go about your day while your car gets detailed.
