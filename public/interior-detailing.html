Professional Interior Detailing in Southwest Virginia

Deep cleaning that makes your cabin feel brand new. Carpet shampoo, leather conditioning, steam cleaning. All done at your location.

What's Included

Fritz's interior detail goes far beyond a basic vacuum. Every surface is cleaned, conditioned, and protected, from the headliner to the floor mats. Seats, carpets, door panels, center console, dashboard, vents, cupholders. Nothing gets skipped. The result is a cabin that looks, smells, and feels like the day you bought it.

We use professional-grade interior cleaners and conditioners that are safe for leather, vinyl, plastic, and fabric surfaces. No harsh chemicals that leave behind a greasy residue or overpowering scent.

Full Vacuum & Debris Removal

Thorough vacuuming of seats, carpets, floor mats, crevices, trunk, and all hard-to-reach areas.

Carpet & Upholstery Shampoo

Hot water extraction and agitation to remove stains, odors, and embedded dirt from fabric surfaces.

Leather Clean & Condition

pH-balanced leather cleaner followed by conditioner to restore softness and prevent cracking.

Steam Cleaning

High-temperature steam kills bacteria, breaks down grime in vents, crevices, and textured surfaces.

Dashboard & Trim Restoration

All plastic and vinyl surfaces cleaned and dressed with UV-protective coating to prevent fading.

Glass & Mirror

Streak-free interior glass cleaning for maximum visibility and a polished look.
