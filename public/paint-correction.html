Machine polishing that removes swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation, restoring your paint to a mirror finish.

What Is Paint Correction?

Paint correction is a multi-step machine polishing process that permanently removes surface defects from your vehicle's clear coat. Swirl marks from automatic car washes, light scratches, water spots, oxidation, and haze are all corrected, leaving behind a deep, glossy, mirror-like finish that looks better than new.

Fritz uses professional dual-action polishers with carefully selected cutting compounds and finishing polishes to safely level the clear coat and eliminate imperfections without removing more material than necessary.

The Process

Thorough Wash & Decontamination

Full hand wash followed by clay bar treatment to remove bonded contaminants that would interfere with polishing.

Paint Depth Measurement

Clear coat thickness is measured to determine safe correction levels, ensuring we never go too thin.

Cutting Stage

Heavier compound removes deeper scratches, swirl marks, and oxidation with a dual-action polisher .

Refining Stage

Finer polish removes any micro-marring from the cutting stage, bringing out maximum gloss and clarity.

Final Inspection

LED inspection lights reveal any remaining defects. Touch-up passes ensure 95%+ defect removal.

Protection

Wax, sealant, or ceramic coating locks in the corrected finish. Pair with ceramic coating for years of protection.
