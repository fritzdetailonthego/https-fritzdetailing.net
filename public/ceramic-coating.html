What Is Ceramic Coating?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds to your vehicle's paint, creating a permanent or semi-permanent layer of protection. Unlike wax that wears off in weeks, a professional ceramic coat lasts for years — protecting your paint from UV damage, chemical stains, bird droppings, tree sap, and road contaminants.

Fritz uses professional-grade SiO2 ceramic coatings that create an incredibly hydrophobic surface. Water, dirt, and grime bead right off, making your vehicle dramatically easier to maintain between washes.

What's Included

Surface Decontamination

Clay bar treatment to remove embedded contaminants, iron fallout, and industrial particles from the paint surface before coating.

Paint Correction

Multi-step machine polishing to remove swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation — the coating locks in a flawless finish.

Ceramic Coat Application

Professional-grade SiO2 ceramic coating applied by hand, panel by panel, with proper cure time between layers.

Hydrophobic Protection

Water beads and sheets off the surface, carrying dirt and grime with it. Your car stays cleaner longer between washes.

UV & Chemical Resistance

Protects against sun damage, bird droppings, tree sap, road salt, and chemical contaminants that eat through unprotected paint.

Years of Durability

Unlike wax that lasts weeks, ceramic coating provides years of paint protection with proper maintenance and care.
